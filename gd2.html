App Design
APP PROTOTYPE
Before / After
Above are some pictures of my finished app design project (there are more pages with a similar feel to these). The assignment here was to create an app that MICA students would get use from. I decided to create an app that would focus and expand on the uses of a student ID card. The app would have features including library check out reminders, spending break downs and of course the student ID.
What I got from doing this assignment was the importance of keeping information and design consistent throughout all the pages. Also, I learned the importance of looking current with apps. i0S7 had just been released by Apple, so in class we discussed their design choices and what to do in order to match their aesthetic. All very important things to consider when designing an app for a mobile device.
PROMO PIECE
Before / After
Besides designing an App, our assignment required that we create a promotional piece to accompany the app. The piece was to be aimed at MICA students in order to get them interested and inform them about the app that I had designed. The first draft (which you can see by clicking the before button) was a simple post card design meant to convey the combination of the MICA student ID with an iPhone 5. However, after doing some research I decided to move forward with a business card like piece which would be double sided.
Student Id cards are about this size as are the Starbucks free iTunes song download cards. As the piece is pretty small, I decided to use a bright blue that would pop. This color also matched the one that I used as the level one background on my app. I combined the student ID and iPhone that I had in my original design and showcased some features that the app had. The backside of the piece was dedicated to students easily being able to download the app.
