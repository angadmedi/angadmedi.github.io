App Design

My first project in Graphic Design 2 ( a class focused about designing for the screen -- phone, computer, tablet) was to design at app that students in MICA would find useful. I decided to create use this opportunity to revision an everyday item for the student body, the student ID.

Besides the App we also had to create a promotional piece that would get inform the student body of the app and get them interested in it. The image on the left is a picture of my final promotional piece. More info on the app design and this piece if you click on it!

Restaurant Website Redesign
Restaurant websites, for the most part, have notoriously bad design. This should be an apparent problem to restaurant owners because more and more people are checking out restaurant websites for directions, hours, a feel for ambiance, menus etc.
For the second project in Graphic Design 2, we had to redesign a restaurant website. We had five burger joints to choose from, so i chose the only one i'd ever been to, Brewer's Art -- I'm new in town.
